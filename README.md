## How to subscribe

Copy a URL above, then follow the steps for your calendar app.

### Google Calendar

1. Open [Google Calendar](https://calendar.google.com) on desktop
2. Click **+** next to "Other calendars" → **From URL**
3. Paste the URL and click **Add calendar**

### Apple Calendar (macOS)

1. Open Calendar → **File** → **New Calendar Subscription…**
2. Paste the URL and click **Subscribe**
3. Set auto-refresh to **Every hour** or **Every day**

### iPhone / iPad

1. Go to **Settings** → **Calendar** → **Accounts** → **Add Account**
2. Tap **Other** → **Add Subscribed Calendar**
3. Paste the URL and tap **Next** → **Save**

### Outlook

1. Open Outlook on the web → Calendar → **Add calendar** → **Subscribe from web**
2. Paste the URL, give it a name, and click **Import**
